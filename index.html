Who We Are

Driving Financial Inclusion for Women

Planned Social Concern(PSC)stands tall as a beacon of hope in Rajasthan, tirelessly serving the underprivileged communities with microcredit and holistic development services. Born in 2006 and taking its first steps into the field in August 2007, PSC has been a steadfast companion to the needy, empowering them with financial resources and nurturing their potential for over a decade. With unwavering dedication, PSC continues to uplift lives, fostering sustainable growth and prosperity in the heart of Rajasthan.

Unveiling Our Journey

Discovering: Our Vision, Mission, and History

These principles guide every decision we make at Planned Social Concern.

Our Mission

Planned Social Concern's mission is to provide access to financialservices to the world's lowest income women enterpreneur to promote micro-enterprises among them in efficient and measurable ways so they can build assets and improve their quality of lives.

Our Vision

Planned Social Concern envisions the day when it serves all people especially women,living in poverty to provide quality financial services while operating on commercial principles of performance and sustainability.

Our History

Planned Social Concern (PSC) was founded in 2006 with seed funding from Grameen Trust, Bangladesh. It later received support from Project Concern International (PCI) and Friends of Women’s World Banking (FWWB), Ahmedabad. Today, MAS Financial Services Limited (MFSL) and Reliance Capital are key lending partners supporting PSC’s growth.

Meet Our Leaders

Board of Directors

Our visionary leaders guiding Planned Social Concern towards a sustainable future.

Rajesh Gupta

Promoter & Director

Mr. Rajesh Gupta is a renowned Chartered Accountant based in Delhi and has been running an audit and tax consultancy firm for over two decades. He has extensive experience in compliance and regulatory matters,assisting clients in evaluating foreign exchange, corporate regulatory, and tax requirements.His areas of expertise include inbound investment advisory, corporate tax planning and compliance, transfer pricing, financial and tax due diligence, statutory and special audits, expatriate taxation, compensation structuring, and addressing tax compliance issues for NGOs. Mr. Gupta graduated from Shri Ram College of Commerce, University of Delhi, and obtained his professional accounting degree from the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India in 1992. He is currently a Fellow Member of the Institute.

Rajvinder Singh

Promoter & Director

Mr. Rajvinder Singh completed his graduation from N.A.S. College, Meerut, in 1974, and obtained his LL.B. degree from D.S. College, Agra University, in 1977. He is a distinguished legal professional with over 35 years of experience practicing before the Delhi High Court and the Supreme Court of India. Over the course of his career, he has developed extensive expertise in diverse areas of law, with a particular interest in matters relating to the social sector. He remains deeply committed to the upliftment of marginalized and underprivileged sections of society. Mr. Singh was appointed as Government Advocate in the Supreme Court of India by the President of India for a three-year term commencing on November 7, 2005. He has also served as retained counsel for various financial institutions and insurance companies, representing them before the Supreme Court of India. At present, he is actively associated with and working alongside several non-governmental organizations, contributing his legal expertise to social and community-oriented initiatives.

Sanjay Gupta

Independent Director

Mr. Sanjay Gupta is a Chartered Accountant (CA) and ICWA professional with over thirty years of experience in his domain. His expertise includes financial accounting, consolidation and finalization of accounts and balance sheets, tax planning, product costing, pricing and cost control, as well as internal, statutory, and management audits. Prior to transitioning to the development sector, he worked with multinational company also. He served many renowned international non-governmental organisations including PCI, Aga khan Foundation etc. In this role, he was responsible for supervising, guiding, and mentoring the finance team; overseeing financial management and control; ensuring compliance with applicable business laws and regulations; managing financial reporting to donors and developing systems and processes for financial operations.

Ravi Gupta

COO & Director

Mr. Ravi Gupta serves as the Director and Chief Operating Officer of PSC. He completed his post-graduation in Rural Management from the Institute of Engineering and Rural Technology, Allahabad, in 1996. With over sixteen years of professional experience, Mr. Gupta has made significant contributions to India’s development sector. He has worked with several national and international non-governmental organizations in various capacities, gaining extensive exposure to grassroots development initiatives. He possesses rich experience and expertise in promoting alternative livelihood opportunities for the poor and slum dwellers across different parts of the country. He has successfully developed and implemented innovative livelihood models, including initiatives supporting families affected by HIV and those impacted by tsunami disasters. Mr. Gupta joined Planned Social Concern (PSC) in August 2007 as Chief Operations Officer. In this role, he is responsible for the overall management and strategic oversight of PSC’s microfinance activities.
