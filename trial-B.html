Who We Are

Driving Financial Inclusion

Planned Social Concern (PSC) stands tall as a beacon of hope in Rajasthan, serving underprivileged communities since 2007. We empower individuals with financial resources and nurture their potential. With unwavering dedication, PSC continues to uplift lives, fostering sustainable growth and prosperity in the heart of Rajasthan.

Unveiling Our Journey

Vision, Mission & History

Our Mission

To provide access to financial services to the lowest income women entrepreneurs, promoting micro-enterprises and improving quality of life.

Our Vision

To serve all people, especially women living in poverty, by providing quality financial services while operating on commercial principles of sustainability.

Our History

Founded in 2006 with seed funding from Grameen Trust. Supported by partners like MAS Financial Services and Reliance Capital.
